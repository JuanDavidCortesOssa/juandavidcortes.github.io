Pirates GO

Pirates GO is a turn-based strategy game whose mechanics, aesthetics, and gameplay simulate those of a conventional board game. In pirates GO you will have to rotate your token around the board until you reach the X on the ground. Along the way you will be able to find enemies with 3 different types of behavior. My role in this project was to create the mechanics, gameplay, objects and a level editor in order to facilitate the creation of each stage.

Polombia with P

This video game is a political satire parodying the presidential years between 2018 and 2022 in Colombia, inspired by the book "Con P de Polombia" by Daniel Samper Ospina. The player must put himself in the shoes of the president of a fictitious nation called "Polombia", in which he will have to manage and make different decisions that will affect his budget, percentage of citizen approval and the support of his political alliances.
